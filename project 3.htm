Beach destinations near Bangalore: Useful list for seniors living in retirement homes in Bangalore

December 3, 2023

 

Categories : Senior Living

 

Bangalore is conveniently located within a reasonable distance from some of India’s most serene beaches, offering the perfect getaway for those in their golden years. As one of the leading senior homes in Bangalore, we share some popular beach destinations that are perfect for seniors:

1. Puducherry Beaches: Puducherry offers a unique blend of French charm and Indian culture. Promenade Beach, also known as Rock Beach, is one of the most popular beaches in Puducherry. The paved promenade provides a magnificent opportunity for seniors to take leisurely walks. They can enjoy sea views and even rest and have delicious snacks at one of the eateries. Auroville Beach is another destination offering a peaceful atmosphere for seniors.

2. Mangaluru Beaches: Mangaluru boasts a range of picturesque beaches. While all of them are beautiful, seniors might particularly enjoy Panambur Beach. It features well-built promenades for leisurely walks and offers the spectacular sight of waves rushing in. The cool sea breeze adds another layer of comfort. Seniors can also admire the mesmerising sunset and savour local seafood delicacies at beachside shacks. Tannirbhavi beach, lined with tall casuarina trees, also offers a scenic and peaceful setting perfect for relaxing strolls.

3. Mahabalipuram Beaches: Mahabalipuram is renowned for its historic monuments and beautiful beaches. Covelong Beach, with its golden sands and azure waters, is a peaceful spot for seniors to relax and bask in the sun. They can also explore ancient temples and stone carvings nearby, adding cultural enrichment to their beach vacation. Also, they can visit Mahabalipuram Beach as it offers a peaceful setting for enjoyable walks and the opportunity to interact with or observe local fishermen.

4. Udupi Beaches: Udupi is known for its pristine beaches and tranquil ambiance. Malpe Beach, featuring golden sands is a top pick for seniors seeking a laid-back experience. The area is well-maintained, making it safe and comfortable for seniors. If time permits, seniors can take a 2-hour ferry ride to St. Marys Island which offers unique rock formations that are a spectacle to behold. Also, seniors can visit Kaup Beach. It provides a scenic view of the coastline from its picturesque lighthouse.

5. Gokarna Beaches: For seniors seeking a more secluded and serene beach experience, Gokarna is an ideal choice. While the trek to beaches such as Om Beach and Kudle Beach may be challenging for some, the tranquillity and untouched beauty make the journey worthwhile. The soothing environment and gentle waves create the perfect setting for relaxation and meditation.
